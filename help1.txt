Inexperienced help
- Login to the program with a username and password.
- If you do not have a username or password press the 'Create new user'.
- Enter a username into the prompt.
- Enter a password into the prompt.
- Then press the button that says 'Submit'.
- You have now created an account and go back to the login page.
- Once you have logged in you can select what task you want to do by clicking the buttons at the top of the screen.
